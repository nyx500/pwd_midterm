Oh boy! I needed to brighten my darker than ink mood ASAP!So choosing this book was the best decision I had lately! Some books might be sold at the drugstores as antidepressants to soothe your anxiety, heal your soul!  This is truly one of them! A brilliant satire! An epic, heart pounding romance! Story in another story premise! An exciting, deliciously riveting action packed adventure! And one of the most brilliant movie adaptation of mid-80’s ! And please listen to its audiobook because sweetest, amazing director of the movie Rob Reiner is the narrator of the book and his acting skills help a lot to savor each moment perfectly!
