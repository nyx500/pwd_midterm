Words of Radiance by Brandon Sanderson(Stormlight Archive,#2) The story, as always with Sanderson, was compelling and filled with an interesting lore and more engaging than the first book in the series. The world Sanderson has created will likely go down as one of the most detailed and expansive fantasy worlds ever created. The story is awesome and well worth reading for anyone who enjoys fantasy with a rich and complex world.Honor is dead. But I'll see what I can do.Phenomenal
