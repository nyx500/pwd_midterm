Welcome to the 1800s Ohio Territory frontier, in a alternative reality where the Americas were not only a haven for religious freedom, but a haven for magical freedom as well. North American colonies have united without revolution, and the those seeking freedom to practice their "knack" push forward to the West.Envisioned as an epic poem based in American folklore, and expanded into a seven-volume saga of Alvin Maker, seventh son of a seventh son, this is the initial story of Alvin, parents and those who come to guide and protect him.
