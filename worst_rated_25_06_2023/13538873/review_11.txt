Highly original page turner with a wonderful mix of dusty bookstores and old bibliophiles, alongside the digital age of Googling masters and venture capitalists, and how both worlds have their ideas and uses in the mystery that emanates from Mr Penumbra's book shop :-)One of those (many?) books written by book nerds, edited by book nerds and published by book nerds to be read by book nerds :) 8 out of 12.2014 read
